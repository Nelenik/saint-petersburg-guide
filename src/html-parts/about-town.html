About City

  • Saint Petersburg is one of the most beautiful cities in Russia, attracting tourists from all over the world with its architecture, cultural monuments, and rich history.

  • The city was founded in 1703 by Peter the Great on the banks of the Neva River and became a symbol of the strength and power of the Russian Empire.

  • Today Saint Petersburg is not only a cultural center but also one of the world's largest ports, serving as an important economic center for Russia.

In this guide, we will tell you about the most interesting places in Saint Petersburg that you should definitely visit when traveling to this wonderful city.
